FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

Try our Investor or Trader plan completely free for up to 7 days. Note that you must cancel your plan before the 7 days are over, or you will be billed once the 7-day trial has ended. Our HODLer plan is completely free with no credit card required.

What are the differences between the plans?

Our free HODLer plan gives basic alerts and is recommended for non-traders that are just looking to hold crypto assets and stay in the loop of what's happening in the market. Our Investor plan gives access to more alerts and is recommended for beginner traders. Our Trader plan gives access to all of our alerts, intended to maximize successful trades when day/swing trading.

What alerts are included with your plans?

Our free plan has access to basic alerts. The investor plan has access to Basic and Advanced alerts. And the Trader plan has access to Premium, Advanced, and Basic. Please refer to our alerts page for more info about each alert.

Does your mobile app only work with iPhone?

Yes. Our mobile app is currently available for iPhone only. For Android users, we have a Telegram bot which requires Telegram to receive our alerts. We are currently working on the Android version of Coinflow. Please foloow us on social media to be alerted when it launches.
