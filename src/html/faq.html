FAQ

Alerts

4 answers
What are Coinflow alerts?

Our alerts are a very powerful tool for any cryptocurrency holders or traders. Our alerts send you instant push notification when certain market conditions are met. These can help you enter/exit your positions for maximum profit potential, even when on the go.

What are the differences between Basic, Advanced and Premium alerts?

Our basic alerts are available completely free, and are intended for crypto holders (non-traders). Use these alerts to stay informed on bigger market moves. Our advanced alerts are on the Investor and Pro plans. These alerts are intended for beginner traders. Our premium alerts are available on the Pro plan and are intended for day/swing traders to maximize their profits in trading crypto.

How do I receive alerts?

After signing up, you'll need to download our iOS app on the AppStore. This is the best experience of Coinflow, as it allows more control over custom notifications. We also have integration via Telegram to receive alerts. Telegram is currently the only way for Android users to receive our alerts on their mobile device, but it also works for iPhone users as well if you prefer this method.

What exchanges are supported?

We currently support every pair on the following exchanges: Binance, Binance US, Bitfinex, Bitstamp & Coinbase Pro. We are working on expanding to other exchanges, please follow us on social media to stay informed when we expand.

Plans

4 answers
How does the free trial work?

Try our Investor or Pro plan completely free for up to 7 days. Note that you must cancel your plan before the 7 days are over, or you will be billed once the 7-day trial has ended. Our HODLer plan is completely free with no credit card required.

What are the differences between the plans?

Our free plan gives basic alerts and is recommended for non-traders that are just looking to hold crypto assets and stay in the loop of what's happening in the market. Our Investor plan gives access to more alerts and is recommended for beginner traders. Our pro plan gives access to all of our alerts, intended to maximize successful trades when day/swing trading.

What alerts are included with your plans?

Our free plan has access to basic alerts. The investor plan has access to Basic and Advanced alerts. And the Pro plan has access to Premium, Advanced, and Basic. Please refer to our alerts page for more info about each alert.

Does your mobile app only work with iPhone?

Yes. Our mobile app is currently available for iPhone only. For Android users, we have a Telegram bot which requires Telegram to receive our alerts. We are currently working on the Android version of Coinflow. Please foloow us on social media to be alerted when it launches.
