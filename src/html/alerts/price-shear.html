About Price Shear

A price shear alert is sent when the price quickly moves a certain percentage above or below the most recently encountered extreme lows or highs of the preceding candles. It is often accompanied by a volume spike.

Price shears are very reactive, so they can alert you to moves in either direction in quick succession. If there is a price breakdown for example, you might receive a negative price shear alert. But if this move turns out to be a fake-out and the price turns around and quickly rises again, our newly improved Price Shear algorithm will detect this much sooner than its previous version, by doing away with the reliance upon a moving average which would be too slow to react to such moves.

As such, the newly improved price shear alert, especially in combination with the overbought / oversold climax alert, can be very useful tools for any trader trying to capitalize on oversold bounces because it will often be one of the first signals that a move in the opposite direction has started.
