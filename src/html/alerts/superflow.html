About SuperFlow

This alert is one of our newest additions and truly excels when it comes to pointing out major inflection points in the markets. It works great for day-traders and swing-traders alike and often gives ample heads-up time to plan and execute a trade before the market starts to turn. Custom designed by the team behind Coinflow, this is an alert that should be in every trader’s tool box.

Using elements from our highly popular climax alert, and by monitoring every supported timeframe simultaneously, we can often pinpoint the exact moment a move in either direction exhausts itself and things start to turn around. It saves traders endless switching between timeframes to do the work of gathering all the data that feeds into this specific indicator.

SuperFlow alerts are sent in real-time and notify you the moment all timeframes below the selected stack level have reached their trigger conditions. Be sure to give this one a closer look and see for yourself how successful it is at finding tops and bottoms with accuracy.
