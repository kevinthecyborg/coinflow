About EMA Cross

This alert fires when two of Crypto’s most significant Exponential Moving Averages (EMAs) cross over either bullish or bearish.

EMA crosses tend to confirm or foreshadow trend changes, therefore they are often used as entry and exit signals when they do occur. They are also very useful for oversold bounce traders because an EMA cross on a shorter timeframe can signal to zoom out to a longer timeframe and watch for the signal to occur there too.
