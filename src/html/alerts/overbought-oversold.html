About Overbought/Oversold

The Overbought/Oversold alert is one of our most popular alerts by far. These signals fire when there are extreme RSI levels, high volume, and both are combined with significant price moves. Designed after our passion for oversold bounce trades, this alert has a very high rate of success and accuracy, giving some great signals even on the longer term timeframes.

But the Overbought/Oversold alert doesn’t just work well for timing entries during oversold conditions, you can use it equally effectively to identify extreme overbought conditions to time your exits. This can provide some very useful signals that you might use in deciding whether to consider selling part or all of your position into strength during the climax. The aim would be to either take profit or to reload again at lower levels, once the market has had a chance to cool off and consolidate.
