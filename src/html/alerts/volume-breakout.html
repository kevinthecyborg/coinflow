About Volume + Breakout

This alert is a combination of the price shear and abnormal volume alerts. Acting as a double validation, this alert only fires when explosive price action is backed by a substantial increase in trade volume. Often firing slightly later than a price shear alert would on its own, it does a good job at confirming that a move is for real and unlikely to have been caused by a single large order as is sometimes the case.
